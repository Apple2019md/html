Intresting facts about Golden Venture.

  • The art form was popularized in 1993 when a ship called the Golden Venture brought illegal Chinese immigrants into USA.
  • While the Chinese refugees applied for political asylum, they were put in prison. To pass their time, the Chinese prisoners folded and assembled paper models.
  • There are very few diagrams for these types of models because, given enough units, you can make almost anything.

Intresting facts about Modular Origami

  • The first modular origami model comes from a 1734 Japanese book by Hayato Ohoka called Ranma Zushiki.
  • Fast-forward some 230 years and the same cube appears in Isao Honda's World of Origami, published in 1965.
